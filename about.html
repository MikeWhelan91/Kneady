Welcome to my Kitchen!

Im so happy you're here. Grab a cookie, take a seat, and let me introduce myself.

Hi guys, my name is Fiona and I'm delighted to welcome you to my website! I'm a self taught baker with a passion for all things sweet. Growing up, I watched my Granny make beautiful jams, breads and scone. Both my parents have been known to dabble in baking and have always been the best support system. Each recipe is tried and tested by those closest to me, making sure that each bake is to the highest standard. I love a challenge and have a creative flair, any idea you may have, I guarantee you I will make it come to life! Looking forward to hearing from you!
